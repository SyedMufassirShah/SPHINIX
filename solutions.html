Featured Solutions

Mission Modernization

The Problem

Legacy technology, inaccessible data, domain siloes, and legacy outmoded constrain the mission in the face of against modern threat actors.

The Solution

The ADEPT Mission Support Model. The ADEPT Mission Support Model. ADEPT Mission Support enables clients to maximize their mission authorities and leverage technology, data, and talent to successfully navigate the modern threat environment.

The Benefit

ADEPT enables clients with a protective mission to adapt their strategies, accelerate operations, and improve techniques, tactics, and procedures (TTPs) to achieve decision and mission dominance.

Demonstrated Results

For a USG customer, Sphinx delivered a complete data automation framework for the integration, processing, and reporting of CI Awareness and Reporting (CIAR) data from 129,000 records, eliminating hundreds of man-hours of manual processing and analysis for real-time mission insight.
