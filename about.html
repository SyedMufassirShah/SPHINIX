Sphinx Origins

Founded in 2018, Sphinx was established by a group of professionals from the Special Operations, Intelligence, and Cyber Warfare Community. We've experienced the challenges of attempting to keep pace with an evolving threat and the real-world implications of failing to do so. With decades of experience conducting multi-domain intelligence and security operations, our team is dynamic, multi-disciplined, and mission-obsessed.
